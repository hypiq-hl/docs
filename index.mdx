# Welcome to HYPIQ

## The Future of On-Chain Trading Intelligence

HYPIQ is the first decentralized prediction protocol built natively on Hyperliquid, allowing users to speculate on the outcomes of real-time trading activity. Instead of guessing election results or sports scores, HYPIQ invites you to engage directly with crypto's beating heart: the market itself.

Users can place outcome-based predictions on whether large positions on Hyperliquid will close in profit (PNL+) or at a loss (PNL−) — transforming passive whale-watching into an interactive, financial game layer on top of real trader behavior.

By leveraging Hyperliquid's high-speed infrastructure and a CLOB-based prediction model, HYPIQ delivers fair, transparent, and high-liquidity prediction markets — powered by the same mechanisms that drive modern trading systems.

## Our Vision
### Make Speculation Transparent, Accessible, and On-Chain

We believe market intelligence should be public infrastructure, not locked inside private systems or institutional dashboards.

In crypto, every trade tells a story — and HYPIQ turns that story into a predictive game open to anyone, anywhere.

While most platforms offer passive analytics, we empower users to participate in the future performance of actual positions — with outcomes settled on-chain, transparently and trustlessly.

HYPIQ is about more than just prediction — it's about building a new class of protocol-native applications that bring meaning, value, and interactivity to blockchain data. It's time to stop watching markets and start engaging with them.

We're building a world where anyone can understand, anticipate, and act on real trading behavior — no hedge fund access required.

**Welcome to the future of on-chain prediction. Welcome to HYPIQ.**
