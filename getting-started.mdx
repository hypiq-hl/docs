# Getting Started

Welcome to HYPIQ! This guide will help you get started with our advanced trading platform.

## Quick Start

Follow these steps to begin trading on HYPIQ:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Sign up for a HYPIQ account at [hypiq.finance](https://hypiq.finance)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Verification">
    Complete the KYC verification process to unlock full platform features
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund Your Account">
    Deposit funds using our supported payment methods
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Trading">
    Begin trading with our intuitive interface and advanced tools
  </Step>
</Steps>
